Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Does this constitute a formal ATO determination?

No. Our assessment produces documentation and gap analysis to support your ATO process — the authorization decision remains with your Authorizing Official. We are not an Authorizing Official and this service does not replace your agency's RMF process.

Which version of the NIST AI RMF do you assess against?

We assess against NIST AI RMF 1.0 (January 2023) with reference to the Generative AI Profile (NIST AI 600-1, July 2024) where applicable. We cross-reference OMB M-24-10 and EO 14110 guidance throughout.

How do you handle classified systems?

We do not process classified information under this service. Assessments are conducted at the unclassified level using system documentation and metadata. For classified environments, contact us to discuss a separate engagement vehicle with appropriate clearance considerations.

What if our AI system is still in development?

Pre-production assessments are highly valuable — they identify compliance gaps before deployment, when remediation is cheapest. Our intake stage distinguishes between deployed and development-stage systems and scopes findings accordingly.

Can this be procured via GSA or SEWP?

Yes. We are registered in SAM.gov and can process orders via GSA Multiple Award Schedule, SEWP V, purchase card, or direct award depending on your agency's procurement requirements. Contact us for a quote formatted for your specific vehicle.

How is the AI (Claude) used in the assessment process?

Claude assists with gap analysis, compliance narrative drafting, and documentation generation based on your assessment responses. All AI-generated content is reviewed, edited, and signed off by a credentialed human assessor before delivery. The assessor is the responsible party for all deliverables — Claude is a force multiplier, not a replacement for judgment.
